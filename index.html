FAQ

How is your menu different from websites created with Wix or Shopify-like website builders?

We make Foodba specifically for cafes and restaurants. This allows us to focus on every little detail, for both guests and venues, that are missing in generic website builders.

Do I need to add photos?

We strongly recommend adding photos. Our research shows that guests skip positions without photos and stop when they see one with a photo. Even photos taken on a phone are better than no photos at all.

Will the menu look the same as in my PDF?

No. A PDF menu is easy to browse with a computer or in print, but not on the mobile.

Do I have to change the QR code if the menu changes?

No, you won't ever need to change the QR code. The QR code will remain the same even if you replace all the items in the menu with new ones.

Can I edit the menu myself?

Yes, of course. You can add, edit, hide and delete both dish sections and menu items. Editing is so simple that anyone who has ever used Google can handle it.

Is the service applicable worldwide?

Yes, you can create your own menu wherever you are. Just sign up by selecting your country and we'll set up the currency.
